Job Hunting

Recruiting Cycles

  • Recruiting cycles are tied to budget cycles
  • Cycles tend to be quarterly
  • Summer and late fall are slower
  • quarterly due to financial reporting requirements
  • vacations & holidays

Internships

  • Mostly late fall/early spring
  • But don’t give up looking

Roles

Job Boards at Conferences

  • Employers recruit at conferences

Saved Searches

  1. Look for jobs on a company’s website
  2. Set up a “saved search” or “alert”

Down-leveling

  • Sometimes employers compromise
  • Sometimes bringing in fresh talent is valuable in itself

Can be because of:

  • lack of talent with the desired experience
  • budget considerations

Consider All Industries

  • Healthcare/Life Sciences
  • Finance
  • Retail
  • Technology
  • Non-Profit
  • Academia

Consider Contract-to-Hire

  • Work as a contractor for 6 months to year
  • Then convert to full-time or find something else

Benefits are tied to the contracting company

Interviews

Embrace In-Person/Hybrid

  • Early career
  • Mentorship

Make it clear you’re okay with in-person. Companies often find on-boarding/mentorship easier in-person.

Practice Coding Interviews

  • HackerRank
  • Advent of Code
  • Beware of interviewing scams

Show Continuous Learning

  • Talk about microlearning or side-projects

Do NOT Lie

  • Or make up answers
  • Instead admit you don’t know, relate to something you do know
